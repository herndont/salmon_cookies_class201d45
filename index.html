Welcome to Pat's Salmon Cookies. Please stop by one of our many locations to get a taste of this Seattle favorite.

These cookies were the brain child of Pat. He started off making them for his dog who loved them. Pat saw a remarkable change in his dog's coat and decided that since he was bald that he had nothing to lose. He started eating them himself and found a new folicle fortitude he had never dreamed of. Instead of selling you these cookies for male pattern baldness he sells them because he thinks they taste great.
